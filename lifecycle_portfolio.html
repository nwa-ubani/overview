Strategic Approach

🎯
Segmentation Logic
  • Company size (1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 200+)
  • Industry vertical (6 key segments)
  • Use case identified at signup
  • Behavioral triggers (feature usage, inactivity)
  • Engagement level scoring
🔄
Automation & Triggers
  • Behavioral email triggers based on product usage
  • Dynamic content blocks per segment
  • A/B testing on subject lines & CTAs
  • Automated pause if user becomes active
  • Win-back branch for inactive users
📊
Success Metrics Tracked
  • Email open rates & click-through rates
  • Feature activation rates per email
  • Time-to-activation improvement
  • Trial-to-paid conversion rate
  • Day 7, 14, 30 retention rates
💡
Key Innovations
  • Personalized video thumbnails with user data
  • In-email interactive elements (polls, calculators)
  • Dynamic progress bars showing trial completion
  • Smart send time optimization per user
  • Cross-channel sync with in-app messaging
